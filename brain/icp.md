# ICP — Procredis

## Tier 1 (Primary)
- **Company type:** Tech companies
- **Size:** 30–200 employees
- **Stage:** Seed to Series B funding round
- **Location:** UK, US, Europe
- **Tech stack:** HubSpot users
- **Budget signal:** Active fundraise, growth phase
- **Pain:** Scaling revenue without bloated headcount, need outbound + RevOps infrastructure

## Tier 2 (Secondary)
- **Company type:** Any B2B (not necessarily tech)
- **Size:** 20–300 employees OR $2M+ raised
- **Location:** Canada, UK, US, Europe, Israel
- **Budget signal:** $2M+ in funding raised
- **Pain:** Same revenue bottlenecks, less tech-mature stack

## Target personas on LinkedIn
- **VP/Head of Sales** — owns pipeline, frustrated with slow outbound and unclean CRM
- **VP/Head of RevOps** — needs systems, drowning in rep requests and manual processes
- **Head of Marketing / Growth** — owns ABM strategy, needs execution infrastructure
- **CRO** — accountable for revenue number, wants predictability and visibility
- **Founder/CEO (20-100 employees)** — wears multiple revenue hats, needs leverage fast

## ICP engagement signal (for tracking)
When logging post engagement, mark a like or comment as "ICP" if it comes from:
- Any of the above titles at a B2B company matching Tier 1 or Tier 2 criteria
- Decision-makers at named accounts we're targeting
- Founders of funded B2B companies
